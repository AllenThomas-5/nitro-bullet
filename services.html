Company Profile

NBI was formed in 2015 and offers industrial cleaning services using Dry-Ice Blasting. NBI uses the latest equipment in the industry and operates the equipment using the utmost safety measures. NBI is one of the first companies in the UAE to use this form of technology in industrial cleaning and as a result has worked alongside high profile private entities over the past 2 years.

NBI uses Dry-Ice Blasting to remove heavy debris( rust, paint, oil, chemical residual precipitations , solidified cement, etc ... ) formed around mechanical machineries, marine, road, rail and aviation structures, all sorts of pipes and electrical equipments and many more which as a result, restores the original performance and saves the client the cost of replacement and the time it takes to replace the parts.

NBI provides highly professional services operated and supervised by qualified engineers to ensure all safety requirements are met and most importantly, ensure that the physical and financial integrity of the machine or structure are not damaged by any way. NBI operates 24/ 7 throughout all the cities of the UAE and therefore can accommodate to tailored requests from the client.

NBI Mission:

Our mission is to deliver superior quality services in industrial cleaning to our valuable customers so as to help them save time, lower costs and to refurbish the structures.
